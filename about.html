About

I'm Mistus Elordie — a satirist and systemic critic writing about the injustice and discrimination of minority groups baked into human society, and the flawed cultural and faux-scientific logic used to justify it.

I am the Founding Editor of The Sofa Sentinels' Gazette — a multi-platform satire publication — and The 3Fs Journal — a personal column documenting the cultural and pseudoscientific origins of discrimination, and the structural changes needed to dismantle them.

My poetry collection, D.I.D I Die & Leave Me Living?, is available on Amazon KDP.
