Mistus Elordie is an advocacy writer, satirist, and multimedia researcher. Operating at the intersection of deep policy research and deadpan systemic critique, they utilize diverse mediums to dismantle the invisible systems that drive the discrimination of minorities in society.

As the Founder of The Sofa Sentinels' Gazette, they leverage structural satire and comedic subversion to challenge status-quo configurations of authority and expose institutional absurdities.
